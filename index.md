*   **Snapping:** Use Entity Snaps (OSNAP) to connect lines precisely at endpoints, midpoints, or centers.
*   **Modifying:** Select an object to move, copy, or rotate it. You can also use the properties pane to change colors or line thicknesses.
*   **Saving Files:** Save your work often. Use the standard .dwg format to maintain compatibility with other design software.
